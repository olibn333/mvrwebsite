Myanmar VR

Myanmar VR brings Virtual Reality technology solutions to Myanmar businesses, NGOs and media, implementing a wide range of applications to enhance sales, increase exposure, and integrate the latest in VR rendering and viewing capabilities.

To Myanmar

Myanmar VR was founded by enthusiasts in 2015 to harness the trend in Virtual Reality technology and bring it to the world’s fastest growing ICT industry. Myanmar has embarked on a transformative political process that promises to radically alter the economy, bringing new technologies and connectivity to millions of citizens previously off-grid.
